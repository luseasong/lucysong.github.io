The Breakfast Buddy
01
OVERVIEW
Background
The Breakfast Buddy is an all-in-one toaster I came up with for a class, DES INV 21: Visual Communication and Sketching. It is a toaster with a built-in alarm clock and kettle for all your morning needs.
02
SKETCHES
The initial sketch
The prompt for this quick sketch was to draw a product we would like to see on the market.
One-Pager
This was the one-pager I created to communicate my idea. We learned skills such as drawing in perspective and how to organize information hierarchies to produce quality one-pagers.
03
SLIDE DECK
One assignment was to create a slide deck as if we were trying to pitch our product. Prior to the assignment, I was taught basic rules for creating decks that I tried to implement in my own.
04
VIDEO
I also had to create a video to communicate my idea. This built upon editing skills I already had, but I enjoyed editing a video that was more of an advertisement style and playing with animation.
