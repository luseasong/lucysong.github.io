The Weather Channel App Redesign
01
OVERVIEW
Background
I start off every morning with a cup of tea and checking the weather. I like to be informed when I put together outfits, plan my day, and just in general. For years, I've been using The Weather Channel's app so when they redesigned the interface in 2018, I was seriously disappointed, and so were many other users.
02
USER RESEARCH
How might we improve the user experience?
I did my own analysis of my own user journery and gathered data from the App Store reviews to pin point main problem areas.
Many people believed the app was too bright and hard to read. Another common complaint was that the information was not displayed in a helpful manner and things were either impossible to find or redundant and did not add to the experience of using the app.
03
PERSONAS
Stephanine is a college student who commutes and has class everyday. She is afraid of getting caught in the rain and being uncomfortable (too hot or cold).
Brian travels a lot for his job and needs to know how to pack for each of his trips to various cities. He can't bring entire wardrobes so he needs to know exactly what to pack based on weather conditions.

JOURNEY MAPS
05
INSIGHTS
Key Insights
1. Users want concise and clear weather information.
2. Many individuals for the bright, white interface difficult to read.
3. Users want relevant weather data.
Goals
My goal was to redesign the interface and reorganize the information hierarchy such to better meet the needs of the user.
06
THE PROCESS
Hi-Fi Prototype
Though I kept the original color scheme, which went well with their logo's colors, I tried to the interface easier on the eyes by using different font weights and colorful icons to direct attention. The first page you see when you open the app tells you the current temperature and weather conditions, but the rest of the page gives a concise overview of the rest of the day, complete with the highest/lowest temperatures as well as wind and weather conditions. If the user wants a more in-depth, hour-by-hour weather report, the Hourly view is easily accessible. Similarly with the weekly view. I also changed the anchor/menu on the bottom to provide clear and direct access the other features of the app such as the news and videos.
07
REFLECTIONS
Closing Thoughts
I would have liked to have been given the opportunity to test my prototype and see if my changes were effective and what else could be improved. Unfortunately, I could not gather enough of my peers to do so.
